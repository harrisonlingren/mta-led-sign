MTA LED Sign Configuration

Make sure to fill out all fields below to set up your MTA LED Sign.

Instructions

  1. Connect your sign to your computer with a USB cable
  2. Fill out all form fields below:
    • Wi-Fi SSID: The name of your Wi-Fi network that the sign will use to connect to the internet (case-sensitive)
    • Wi-Fi Password: Your Wi-Fi network's password (case-sensitive)
    • MTA Station: Use the search to find the subway station you want to monitor, and make sure to select the right subway line
    • Train direction: Choose to display departure times for trains heading either North or South
  3. Click "Make config", and save the generated secrets.py file to your sign. Your sign should show up like a flash drive, labeled CIRCUITPYTHON
